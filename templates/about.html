Our Mission

Lotus was created to make women’s health and wellbeing support more accessible, inclusive, and stigma-free — especially for students. It’s a private, always-on companion offering guidance, encouragement, and trustworthy information.

Meet the Creator

Hi! I’m Harshali, the creator of Lotus. As a student, I realized how hard it can be to ask for help — especially when it comes to topics like periods, anxiety, or hormones. Lotus is my way of offering a safe space, backed by tech and care.

How It Works

  • 💬 You Ask – A question about health, mood, or support.
  • 🔍 Lotus Understands – It uses natural language to interpret your intent.
  • 🧠 It Searches – Medical databases like MedQuAD & DrugBank (with strict fallback rules).
  • ✅ You Get Support – Friendly, non-judgmental info & advice.

Where We're Going

We're planning to launch a mobile version, add local language support, introduce cycle syncing, and expand to more universities. Lotus is growing — just like you.

Privacy & Safety First

No personal data is saved. Lotus provides information, not diagnosis. All responses are generated ethically and filtered for sensitive care.

🌸 Core Services Offered by Lotus

General advice on menstrual health, pregnancy, fertility, contraceptives, and hormonal health.

Non-diagnostic support for symptoms (e.g., “What could cause lower abdominal pain?”).

Details on common medications like paracetamol, birth control pills, etc.

Side effects, dosage guidelines, and usage warnings.

Caution for interactions with female-specific medications.

Tips for stress relief, emotional well-being, and mindfulness.

Guidance on PMS mood swings, postpartum depression, anxiety.

Encouragement and supportive resources.

General info on tracking, irregularities, and hygiene.

Answers common health-related FAQs.
